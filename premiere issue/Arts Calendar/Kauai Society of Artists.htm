 Art Kauai 2012, Kauai’s most important juried art show, opens on Friday night August 3rd 2012 at 5:30 at the Kauai Society of Artists’ art Gallery at Kukui Grove Shopping Center. The public is invited to the opening. Awards will be presented by the juror and refreshments will be served. 76 works were selected from 54 artists by Juror, David Belkhe, who will do a walk and talk and discuss the show. The show will be open seven days a week from 11 am to 5pm and 8pm on Fridays. The show will remain open to the pubic till September 11th 2012.
