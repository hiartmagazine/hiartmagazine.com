Events & Exhibitions

 

"The New Black" - Black & White Photography Exhibition

March 9 - May 3
Juror: Darrell Orwig
In our modern world of fast moving images and bold colors we seldom take a moment to pause and look deeper at the form and rhythm of the world around us. The goal of this unique juried exhibition is to take this pause and explore beyond the realm of color into shades of gray from the deepest blacks to the brightest whites.

 

The Hui Teaches: Student & Faculty Salon
Receiving day: May 7

Exhibition dates: May 18 - July 5, 2013
The Hui's gifted students and teaching artists blend their diverse talents to present work created over the past two years. Curated by the core faculty of each art department, this exhibition showcases the skills, range, and spirit of the Hui's artistic community. Prospectus & entry form here.


2013 Solo Artist Exhibition
Featuring Jonathan Yukio Clark & Chenta Laury

August 10 - September 20, 2013
The Solo Artist Exhibition is one of the only exhibitions in HI-state that provides the unique opportunity for artists to be personally involved in all the facets of exhibition planning and installation. It has advanced careers, spurred community dialogue, and continues to define our local visual arts movement. Learn about past artists here.
