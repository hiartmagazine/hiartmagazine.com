Photoshop Layers Class at Keaau Fine Art Center!

Unleash Your Creativity with Layers!

This class is a comprehensive study of Layers and how to use them to your best advantage. Eliminate confusion about this extremely valuable tool in Photoshop (and many other image editing applications). Once you fully understand Layers you'll wonder how you ever lived without them!

More info and signup

This class will cover Photoshop CS5 (most techniques apply to some previous versions):

  • Layer Stacking
  • Layer Naming
  • Layer Groups
  • Layer Blend Modes
  • Layer Blending Options
  • Layer Opacity
  • Layer Styles
  • Layer Locking
  • Adjustment Layers
  • Layer Masks
  • Smart Objects
  • Text Layers
  • Layer Comp

Bring note-taking materials or your laptop computer. We will provide tables and power to all attendees who wish to follow along with their computer.

Cost: Just $29 for 3 hours of valuable instruction.
