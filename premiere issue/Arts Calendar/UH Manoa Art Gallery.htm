Bachelor of Fine Arts Exhibition 2012
April 22 - May 11, 2012
(Awards Ceremony on Sunday, April 22 at 2:00 p.m. followed by the opening reception from 3:00 to 5:00 p.m.)

The artwork in Bachelor of Fine Arts Exhibition 2012 attests to the academic and studio achievements of the imminent graduates in the Department of Art and Art History at the University of Hawaii at Manoa. Students in the BFA program expand the boundaries of traditional media, and explore of concepts that give rise to unconventional and innovative approaches. View the work of final year Bachelor of Fine Arts students in an exhibition showcasing talent and skilled practice in a variety of media.

Gallery hours: Monday - Friday 10:30 - 5:00, Sun. 12:00 - 5:00. Closed holidays and Saturdays. Free admission. Parking fees may apply.
