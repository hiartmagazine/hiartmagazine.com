Classes Starting Soon!

Introduction to Acrylic Painting
Roger B. Stephens
Tuesdays, 5 weeks: January 4 - February 1, 6:00 pm - 9:00 pm
In this class, participants will be introduced to the remarkable possibilities of painting with a water-based painting medium. Paints, brushes, substrates, styles and themes will be discussed and demonstrated. Roger has 35 years of experience with acrylics and students are sure to gain significant skill with the use of this amazing material.
Fee: $162 ($203 nonmember)
Supply list provided upon registration.

Jewelry Making I
Julie Matheis
Wednesdays, 6 weeks: January 5 - February 9, 6:00 pm - 9:00 pm
One of the Hui's most popular classes, students cut, drill and pierce metal, learn various soldering techniques, and bezel set a stone with projects that may include pendants, bracelets, earrings, and/or rings. This class is highly recommended before any other jewelry classes may be taken. Copper and brass sheet metal and wire are included in the supply fee and sterling silver will be available for purchase.
Fee: $195 ($244 nonmember)
Supply Fee: $35

Introduction to Fashion Illustration
Lovisa Oliv
Thursdays, 6 weeks: January 20 - February 24, 6:00 pm - 9:00 pm
This new course teaches the basic skills for fashion drawing and design. Students will practice sketching and developing their own fashion designs on a fashion figure. The class will give the students a feel for what is required of a fashion designer and prepare the students that may want to pursue a career in fashion.
Fee: $175 ($219 nonmember)
Supply list provided upon registration.

Painting with Pixels: Adobe Photoshop Techniques for Teens
Rainelle Lushina
5 weeks: NEW DATES: Jan 13 - Feb 10, 4 pm - 6 pm
Transform photos and scanned pieces into fantastic works of art! Mimicking the masters, we will experiment with pop art and surrealism, pointillism and digital collaging. Students will create a self-portrait in the style of Andy Warhol, learn to turn an ordinary snapshot into a surreal scene, discover the effects of texture, and playfully create a concept-driven composition with images and text.
Fee: $95 ($119 nonmember)
Supply Fee: $25

Call to Artists
 
Solo Exhibition 2011: Create Your Own Show!
Proposals Due - January 10, 2011
June 18 - July 23, 2011
Opening July 17
Hui No`eau accepts applications annually for artists to exhibit a complete body of work in the Hui gallery.  The Solo Exhibition is one of the only exhibitions in the state that provides the unique opportunity for artists to be personally involved in all facets of exhibition planning and installation of artwork. Learn more about Hui exhibitions, events, rentals, and art classes for adults and children at www.huinoeau.com or phone (808) 572-6560.
 
Hui No'eau is open Monday through Saturday, from 10-4:00.  Located at 2841 Baldwin Ave., one mile below Makawao town. Please visit our website at www.huinoeau.com for more information on classes and exhibitions.
