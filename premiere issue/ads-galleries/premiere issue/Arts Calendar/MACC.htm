November 10 - December 23, 2010
Ho‘oulu: The Inspiration of Hula

MACC is pleased to collaborate with the Hawai‘i State Foundation on Culture and the Arts to present an exhibit of hula-inspired and hula-related works from the Art in Public Places Collection. The exhibit will include portraits of loea hula (hula resources) by Photographer Shuzo Uemoto as well as paintings of renowned kumu hula who have received the Order of Distinction for Cultural Leadership from the State of Hawai‘i: ‘Iolani Luahine, Rosalie Lokalia Montgomery, Mary Kawena Pukui and Edith Kanaka‘ole. Several Maui families from uninterrupted genealogical lines of hula have been invited to display their costumes, adornments and implements of hula.
