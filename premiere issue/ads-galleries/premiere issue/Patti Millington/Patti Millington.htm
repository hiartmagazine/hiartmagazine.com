Patti Millington creates her Mechanical/Organical constructions by combining digitally based collages with found objects. Through techniques she has developed, she translates her digital images from the virtual realm into three dimensions, giving them life…her life at 40. The issues she portrays reflect the universal human need to come to terms with the past, embrace the future, and consider mortality; to throw out the garbage, and build on the good stuff. Mechanical/Organical is talking about newfound freedoms and concerns. Millington's mechanical images are digitally produced from photographs she took of rusting machinery, clockworks, and scientific equipment. She remakes them "organical" by giving them a human, feminine story.

 

 
BENEATH THE SKIN     2007                               14"h x 22"w x 8"d                                   digital & mixed media		 "Beneath The Skin" is a self portrait depicting a woman comfortable in her skin and beneath it. She has unlocked her voice, and opened the door to her heart. Finally she sees herself without the expectation of perfection.

 


FLOOD GATES     2007     8"h x 8"w x 2"d     digital & mixed media


                                                                                view 2
 
"Flood Gates" conveys a sentiment that is identifiable for most. A closer look reveals the numbers 4 and 0 almost subliminally etched in the rusty metal, a statement by the artist of her own relation to the subject.		  

 

 
DEFENSE     2007                  11"h x 12"w x 8"d              digital & mixed media       

  

detail                                                          detail
   

"Defense," for Millington, is reminiscent of the jewelry box of her childhood with the twirling ballerina within. Here, the dancer is transformed into a martial artist, protected by a force-field. She moves under the mechanical power of the hand, the heart, the mouth, and the foot as means of self defense, establishing boundaries.

  

 

 


FINDING OUR WAY    2007   5"h x 6"w x 8"d   digital & mixed media


                                                                                   view 2

 
"Finding Our Way" is an exploration of micro and macro. The view through the magnifying lens, reveals an atom which appears as a miniature version of the solar system depicted on an antique map. From the invisibly small to the inconceivably large, neither can be seen in all its detail, but Millington proposes their similarities.
 		  

 


THE WORKS     2006                                                            12"h x 36"w x 2"d                                                             digital & mixed media

"The Works" incarnates the life cycle of a human being, through the artist's own mementos. Birth, childhood, graduation, loss of loved ones, the subject of death. Layered images sandwiched between sheets of plexiglass lend a three dimensional feeling to Millington’s big machine of existence. The use of transparencies allows light to shine through some of the landmarks of her life.

Mechanical/Organical is a point of view - a position regarding time and life experience. Patti Millington's poignant confabulations systematically transport us within and without, back and forth from a place she has simultaneously discovered and invented.
