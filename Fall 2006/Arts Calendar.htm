ULUIWEHI GUERRERO: THE HEART OF HULA
Saturday, September 9

Uluwehi Guerrero's concert marking 25 years of teaching hula. An internationally acclaimed Kumu Hula and Na Hoku Hanohano award winning recording artist, Uluwehi will be joined with over 100 students from his Halau Hula Kauluokala.
Maui's own Ambassador of Aloha presents an exciting evening filled with beautiful Traditional and Contemporary Hawaiian Music and Hula. Come celebrate this special cultural event! Tickets: TBA
