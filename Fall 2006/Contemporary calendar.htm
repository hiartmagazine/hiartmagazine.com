24/11: art down the hill
Thursday, May 18, 2006, 7pm / At NextDoor, 43 N. Hotel St.
24/11 combines video performance, live film scoring and dj's, adding to Chinatown's hip nightlife. Experience the Neistat Brothers', straight from NYC, for a video performance that will knock you out. In addition, Jerome James & Band will do a jazzy score to a remixed version of Orson Welles' silent film "Four Men on a Raft," and turntable magic by DJ Keith. Celebrate art outside the gallery walls with 24/11! 21+, $5 members / $10 non-members. Space is limited. For pre-sale tickets call (808) 526-1322 x10.
