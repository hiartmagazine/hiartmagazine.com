Regarding my Work

I am adding to the discourse on sexuality, altered states and the fantastical.

The element of the unexpected and the tenuous, fragile nature of existence, along with the desires we ceaselessly encounter, the barrage of change, all this is behind the work I do.

Besides being about myself, it is also a critique on society. It asks for more tolerance and promotes a “freer” environment, going against conservative trends. I depict that which is restricted, prohibited and repressed.

I am interested in people, their perceptions and bodies. Sexuality, the grotesque, the abnormal, changed states of mind/ being, transformation and the fantastical all come into play. The drama or lack of it that goes on within the individual, the couple, the group and the crowd are subjects of mine; people sharing spaces and the dynamics between them.

The viewer is seeing what could be going on in the mind of someone else. They could be consciously using their imagination to “color” the situation, be in the midst of an altered state, exist in an alternate world or be dreaming. I am curious about underlying currents, hidden thoughts, desires and actions that go on within an individual. I examine the contrast between ones’ internal and external lives, and the mixing of fantasy with reality.
