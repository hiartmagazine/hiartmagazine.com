Biographies and Custom Websites

Images and the written word form the window through which an international audience is introduced to your work. In a world of ubiquitous information, creative use of language and web sites can conjure evocative images, eliciting credibility, and a lucid link to collectors, dealers and curators.

With thirty years writing experience in publications from magazines and books to newspapers, museums and the movie industry, we will take the time to listen to you, understand your motivations and intentions and express them in the way curators and publishers appreciate.

Specializing in constructing unique websites and creative biographical writing, we support you in presenting to the public, the unique body of work, motivations and intentions which are your distinct, unique signature.

Exhibition Entries

As more and more organizations elect to utilize online services like CaFE to manage entries for exhibitions, formatting requirements and uploading procedures can be daunting even for the technically adept. There's no reason to miss out on exciting opportunities to exhibit your art; we can assist with image preparation, uploading and completion of the sometimes challenging entry process.
