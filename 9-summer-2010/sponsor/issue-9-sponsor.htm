ART EDITION 2010 will take place in Bexco, Busan, Korea on October 6th - 10th, 2010. Since its beginning in 1995, Seoul International Print Photo Art Fair has been the only art fair for Print and Photography in Korea. About sixty domestic and international galleries participate, providing an opportunity to see art markets of modern printmaking, photography, media art and many other works.


‘Busan Biennale’ and ‘Busan International Film Festival’ will be held during the same time as ART EDITION.


Participating countries include: Korea, Japan, Hong Kong, China, Germany, Spain, Netherlands, Italy, India, Australia, France, Taipei and Luxemburg.
