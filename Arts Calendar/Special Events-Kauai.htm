Hawai‘i Craftsmen’s 42nd Annual Statewide Juried Exhibition. Open to artists and craftsmen residing in the State of Hawai‘i. Accepting traditional and non-traditional crafts with a fresh approach or point of view. Eligible media include: clay, fiber, glass, metal, mixed media, and wood. Both functional and non-functional works are eligible. Entries must be of original design, created independently within the last 2 years, and not previously exhibited in a juried exhibition. Cash awards available. Exhibition scheduled Oct. 10-31, at the Academy Art Center. This year’s juror is Helen Drutt of the Helen Drutt: Philadelphia, a gallery devoted to contemporary craft. In-person jurying will be done on each island (except Lanai and Molokai). Submission drop-off deadline: SEPT. 30, from 11 a.m.-1 p.m., at Kauai‘i Marriott Resort & Beach Club, Puna Ballroom, 3610 Rice St., Lihu‘e. Entry fee for members: $25 for first 2 entries, $10 for each additional. Entry fee for non-members: $65 for first 2 entries, $10 for each additional. For more information, contact Verena Snively at (808) 203-9864, or check website: www.hawaiicraftsmen.org.

Kaua‘i Visual Arts - Workshops and Classes

Kauai Academy of Creative Arts Fall Intersession Program. Offering a program in the visual and performing arts for ages 6 and up (must be enrolled in 1st grade). The one-week program will offer experiences in music, dance, and the visual arts. All students will participate in “Jammin’ with Sam!,” to learn techniques and skills using musical instruments; “Hip Hop!,” a funky dance class; and “Paint, Scratch N Draw,” simple lessons in creative utilization of color, line and form. Scheduled Oct. 5-9, from 8 a.m.-12 noon, at the Island School in Puhi. Enrollment is limited. Tuition: $115. Financial assistance in available. For more information, contact: P.O. Box 481, Lihue, Hawaii 96766, Phone: 346-7586, or check website: www.kauaicreativearts.org.
