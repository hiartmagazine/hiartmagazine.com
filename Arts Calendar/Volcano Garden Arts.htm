Artists in Action
Saturday, May 22nd, 2010
11 am - 3 pm
Join the Volcano Village Artists Hui for the annual "Artists in Action" day of free art demonstrations and hands-on workshops! Explore a variety of mediums with first rate artisans, such as glass blowing, pottery, metal tooling, relief printmaking, collage, Japanese paste paper and ferns-on-fabric textile painting. This event is free and people of all ages are encouraged to participate!
Cafe Ono will be serving a delicious gourmet organic plate lunch throughout this event.
Please call 985-8979 or visit our website for more information about Artists in Action and our other upcoming events.

Come for a fruitful evening of sharing words! Bring your own poems or another poet's work that you would like to share with the group. This

The 2nd Annual Volcano Pottery Sale
Saturday, June 12th, 2010
10 am - 4 pm
Volcano Garden Arts presents the 2nd Annual Volcano Pottery Sale! Local potters Ron Hanatani and Emily Herb will be on hand showcasing a wide variety of their latest creations. This is a great chance to meet the artists and browse through a large assortment of pottery at affordable
prices. Wares on display will include high fired functional stoneware, tableware, vases, planters and more sculptural pieces and works inspired
by native Hawaiian birds and plants.
A delicious gourmet plate lunch will be available for purchase at Cafe Ono throughout this event.
Please call 985-8979 or visit our website at volcanogardenarts.com for more information about this wonderful event.
