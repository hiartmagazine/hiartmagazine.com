FACES OF HAWAII 2010
EXHIBITION OPENING & BOOK DEBUT:  November 28th at idspace

_________________________________________________________
 

In 2008, HI Art Magazine invited the residents of Hawaii to create a portrait of the time, place and community in which we live, through the faces of its inhabitants. The end result is this exhibition and book, curated by Darrell Orwig, artist and Director of the Schaefer International Gallery at the Maui Arts and Cultural Center.

The gallery exhibition will run through the month of December 2008 at idspace, on the Big Island. Please call  966-8943 for an appointment.

The book, Faces of Hawaii, is 8 1/2"x 11", 56 pages, with 41 full color portraits. It is available in soft cover and hard cover through the on-line store in this magazine.
