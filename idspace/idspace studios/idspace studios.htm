idspace studios
 


   
In our fully equipped, professional studio in Kurtistown, Hawaii we offer pottery classes for beginners through advanced students, with instruction in wheel throwing, hand building, press molding, underglaze, and overglaze decoration.    
 

   
   
 
firing
For those who like to work from home or in your own studio, we offer kiln space, glazes, clays and tool kits for sale. We also sell a wide variety of stains and underglazes.


clay
We always stock high-quality Cone 10 clays from Aardvark and can custom order for you regularly.

We currently have:

Hopkins White
Coleman Porcelain
Rio Red
Jamaica (dark brown)
 

studio rental
We rent studio space by the hour and offer one-on-one instruction for students of all levels.

 discounts
Discounts for family members, referrals, and continuing students are available.
