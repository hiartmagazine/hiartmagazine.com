Shizuno Nasu / contemporary dancer

Born in Osaka, Shizuno Nasu started her classical ballet training at the age of three. She was chosen as the exchange student with Bolshoi ballet when she was seven. At the age of nineteen, she has started her career as a professional dancer.　Since then, she has performed widely both in Japan and abroad.  Shizuno Nasu resides in Volcano Village on the Big Island of Hawaii.
