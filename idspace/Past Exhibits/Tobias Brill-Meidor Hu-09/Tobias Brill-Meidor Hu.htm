I'm on the yellow brick road
I'm down the rabbit hole
I'm in never never land

Tobias Brill

There exists a psychological aspect that chronicles the wanderings of my mind and delves into the realm of magical realism. The work is in part a meditation and research into hard to explain occurrences that happen outside of rationality.

I work "brain to paper" using intuition, being open, direct, and taking chances. Intense situations are depicted. They are libidinal, grotesque and other worldly. The dark side of life is the ordinary. An atmosphere of danger and freedom exists. There is a battle between good and evil, beauty, and power. I find myself within the lines, in the midst of the ongoing process of transformation and evolution.
