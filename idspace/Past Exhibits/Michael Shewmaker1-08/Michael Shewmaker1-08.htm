Michael Shewmaker
Obiter Dictum

 
 click on pictures to enlarge
 

 
Multifaceted Big Island artist, Michael Shewmaker, is primarily known for his large format photography. In the early seventies he studied metalsmithing at Southern Illinois University and worked as a blacksmith for several years. In his current exhibition of sculpture at idspace, he returns to his roots.

In 2007, Shewmaker was accepted for the Seventh Annual Florida Outdoor Sculpture Competition, sponsored by The Polk Museum of Art in Lakeland, Florida. His work entitled 'Volute' is installed there for one year.

His current series of work entitled ‘Obiter Dictum’ will be on display at idspace from January 6th to February 29th, 2007.
