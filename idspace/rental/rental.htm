An Art & Plant Lover's Paradise

idspace is located in a tropical botanical garden
setting in Kurtistown on the Big Island of Hawaii.


 
  Stretch out your id in this spacious apartment, with complete kitchen, office and private bathroom, nestled within a 3 acre sculpture garden, art gallery and working artist's studio. Plant lovers will delight in an ever-changing variety of breath taking exotic blooms, and artistic inspiration can be found around every corner. The gallery features bi-monthly exhibitions of contemporary art from Hawaii artists and the professional ceramics studio is the source of works by artists Stephen Freedman and Jamie Stokes as well as their students. 

All of the art in the rental space is by Hawaii artists and is available for purchase. The idspace Gallery Shop features an even greater assortment of art and gift items, all by local artists. Meetings and visits to featured artist's studios can also be arranged.
