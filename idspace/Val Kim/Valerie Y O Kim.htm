Recent openings for exhibitions at our tiny venue have been inundated by interested viewers. Due to limited parking, from now on those who RSVP first may attend the initial opening, while subsequent respondents will be invited to a second artist-hosted event on a later date. The gallery will continue to be open by appointment to everyone during the course of exhibitions. This way we can continue to offer a great experience, but manage the logistics and expenses associated with our venue which runs almost entirely upon donations and our own funds.
